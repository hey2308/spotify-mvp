# Spotify AI Listening Paths – MVP PRD

## Project Overview

Design and build an AI-native MVP for Spotify Growth that helps users discover meaningful new music with minimal effort.

The MVP introduces **AI Listening Paths**, powered by an AI decision engine called **Spotify Discovery DNA**.

The experience should feel like a natural extension of Spotify's existing Queue experience rather than a redesign of the player.

---

# Problem Statement

Spotify has one of the world's most sophisticated recommendation systems, yet many users continue to listen to familiar songs, playlists and artists.

Research indicates that users are willing to discover new music but rarely make an active effort to search for it. Instead, they expect Spotify to understand their current listening context and introduce relevant music naturally.

Current recommendation systems primarily rely on historical listening behaviour and collaborative filtering. While effective at predicting long-term preferences, they often reinforce existing habits and cannot fully understand the user's intent in the current listening session.

The opportunity is to build an AI layer that understands the user's **current context** rather than only historical preferences.

---

# Core AI Capability

## Spotify Discovery DNA

Spotify Discovery DNA is an AI decision engine that continuously predicts the user's current listening context.

Instead of relying only on listening history, it combines:

* Current song
* Listening history
* Current listening session
* Time of day
* Day of week
* Weather
* User's usual activity during similar sessions (commute, gym, work, study, evening relaxation, etc.)
* Device type
* Discovery readiness
* Skip behaviour
* Repeat behaviour
* Session length
* Search behaviour
* Historical taste profile

The output is a **Current Session Context**, for example:

* Morning Commute
* Workout
* Evening Relaxation
* Deep Focus
* Weekend Exploration
* Late-night Chill

This AI engine is invisible to users and powers every listening path.

# User-facing Feature

## AI Listening Paths

The feature appears inside the Queue screen.

Immediately below **Next in Queue**, display an AI-generated section titled:

# AI Listening Paths

**Subtitle**

Choose how your listening journey continues.

Users can either:

* Select one listening path
* Ignore all options

If ignored, Spotify continues exactly as it does today.

# Hover Interaction (Desktop)

When the user hovers over any Listening Path card:

Expand the card smoothly.

Display:

* First **3 upcoming songs** in that path
* Album artwork
* Song name
* Artist
* Estimated queue duration
* Small explanation generated by AI

Example:

> **Built for your evening commute based on today's weather, your current session and your listening habits.**

Display a CTA:

**Choose this path**

Hover should not immediately change the queue.

It is only a preview.

---

# Mobile Interaction

Since hover is unavailable on mobile:

Tapping once on a Listening Path expands it.

The expanded card displays:

* First 3 upcoming songs
* Queue duration
* AI explanation
* Choose Path button

Second tap selects the path.

# AI Behaviour

**Do NOT build an actual recommendation algorithm.**

Mock all AI outputs.

Assume Spotify Discovery DNA receives the following inputs:

### User Signals

* Current song
* Listening history
* Saved songs
* Followed artists
* Genre affinity

### Session Signals

* Recently played songs
* Session duration
* Skip behaviour
* Repeat behaviour
* Search behaviour
* Queue interactions

### Context Signals

* Time of day
* Day of week
* Weather
* Device type
* Location category (home, commute, gym — inferred, not explicit)
* User's usual activity during similar sessions

### Behaviour Signals

* Discovery readiness score
* Novelty tolerance
* Familiarity preference
* Exploration history

---

The AI should infer:

* Current Session Context
* Discovery Readiness
* Preferred Listening Intent

Then generate:

* Stay in the Vibe
* Discover Something New
* Fit This Moment

Each path should include:

* Queue title
* First 3 songs
* Queue duration
* One-line AI explanation

---

# Design Principles

* Keep Spotify's visual language.
* Do not redesign the player.
* Make AI feel assistive, not intrusive.
* The feature should require **zero mandatory interaction**.
* Users should always be able to ignore AI and continue listening normally.
* Every AI decision should be explainable through a short, human-readable reason.
* Prioritize trust, transparency, and low cognitive load.
